🚧 ARKA Montessori Preschool breaks ground in Leander
The Gist. TX Sparks Construction has held a groundbreaking ceremony for ARKA Montessori Preschool, a new early childhood education facility coming to Leander. The project is now under construction.
Why it matters. As Leander continues to grow, new preschool options give families more choices for their young children's education and care in the area.

🍽️ Mouton's Bistro & Bar · Cajun & Creole
Where. 1821 S Lakeline Blvd Suite 104, Cedar Park, TX 78613
