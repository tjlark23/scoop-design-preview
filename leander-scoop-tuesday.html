  Local Lowdown 
 
🚧 183A toll expansion clears final design review
The Gist. The Central Texas Regional Mobility Authority approved final designs for the 183A extension north through Leander, adding two lanes and a new direct-connect ramp at San Gabriel Parkway. Construction is slated to begin this fall.
Why it matters. It's the single biggest commute change for north Leander in years — expect lane shifts through 2027, but a faster run to the Cedar Park job centers when it's done.
